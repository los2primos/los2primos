Sobre nosotros

Los 2 Primos have become one of the favorite hamburger & hot-dog restaurants by the UACh students and the Texcocan families in very short time. Always bringing the highest and freshest ingredients combining them with our Mexico north-style seasoning.

Founded by two entrepreneur cousins passionate about food and cooking who were once looking to create the hamburgers that everyone would admire.

Los 2 Primos love to try new things and experiment new flavours updating our menú every 6 months and loving how the customers enjoy trying out our innovative products which always have our distinguished style
